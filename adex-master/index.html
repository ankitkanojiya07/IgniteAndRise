About IgniteAndRise

IgniteAndRise is dedicated to directing individuals to the right career opportunities and providing comprehensive HR solutions to businesses. With a focus on both B2B and B2B2C models, we aim to serve as a trusted partner for clients and candidates alike. Our services span across recruitment, HR management, policy formulation, and strategic HR consulting, By guiding startups and established companies, we foster growth and excellence in the market.
